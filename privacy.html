Finance & Assets Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Finance & Assets, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your name, email address, contact information, and transaction history when you use our platform. In addition, we collect non-personal information like browser type, IP address, and device details to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us enhance our services, process transactions, and deliver personalized content. We also use it for security verification, fraud prevention, and to comply with legal obligations. Finance & Assets never sells or rents personal data to third parties.

Data Protection and Security

We employ advanced encryption, firewalls, and secure server technology to protect your data. Access to your information is limited to authorized personnel who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements. Despite these measures, no online system is entirely immune from risk, and we encourage users to take steps to safeguard their accounts.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also opt out of marketing communications by updating your account preferences. For inquiries or to exercise your data rights, please contact our Privacy Officer via the contact page.
