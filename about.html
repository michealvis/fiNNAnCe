Needs to know ↧

Finance and assets are closely linked concepts that form the foundation of economic and business management. Finance refers to the management of money, investments, and other financial instruments to achieve specific goals, such as growth, stability, or profit. Assets, on the other hand, are valuable resources owned by an individual, company, or organization—such as cash, property, equipment, stocks, or digital currencies like Bitcoin—that can generate income or be converted into cash. Together, finance and assets represent how wealth is created, maintained, and utilized to ensure financial health and sustainable growth.
